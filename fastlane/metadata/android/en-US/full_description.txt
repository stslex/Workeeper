Gym Progress Tracker is a simple and convenient app for those who want to systematically improve their workouts. 

📋 Features:
• Log exercises, sets, reps, and weights.  
• Review history and analyze progress.  
• Visualize development with charts and statistics.  
• Minimalist design – nothing extra, just your workouts.  

🏋️ Who is it for:
• Athletes who track their performance.  
• Beginners who want structured training.  
• Everyone who values discipline and order in the gym.  

💡 All data is stored locally on your device.  
No registration required, no personal data collected.
